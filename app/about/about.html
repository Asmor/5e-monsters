Who is Fantasy Computerworks?

Fantasy Computerworks is a team of two developers, Axel and Adam, who like keeping small slices of the internet running.

Other useful tools we've created:
  • Fantasy Calendar - The best tool for RPG calendar creation on the Internet. (We're not biased, we're just the creators. Promise!)
  • Dungeon Generator - A random dungeon generator using beautiful tiles by Kristian Richards
  • FoundryVTT Sequencer - A FoundryVTT module that implements a basic pipeline for managing the flow of a set of functions, effects, sounds, and macros.

Fantasy Computerworks is not associated with Wizards of the Coast.
